How Jake on the Lake started

Born on a Pier. Built for Weddings.

It started in 2020 with two acoustic guitars, a tambourine, and the end of a wooden pier. Jake and his dad — The Other Guy — played covers from their lakeside dock in Pembroke Pines. By the third week, neighbors were paddling kayaks over. By the second month, more than 200 people were anchoring boats off the seawall every Saturday.

Five years later, the lineup has grown to a five-vocalist Americana ensemble with two original singles on Spotify, Apple Music & Amazon — and a working repertoire of country, rock & pop tuned for everything from a brewery courtyard to a vineyard wedding in the Blue Ridge.

"We never planned a band. We planned a Saturday on the water. The band came to us." — Jake Valdes, founder & lead

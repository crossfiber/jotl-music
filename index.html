How Jake on the Lake started

Born on a Pier. Built for Weddings.

It started in 2020 with two acoustic guitars, a tambourine, and the end of a wooden pier. Jake Valdes and his dad Danny, who quickly picked up the nickname "The Other Guy" because nobody could ever remember which of the two played which part, traded covers from their lakeside dock in Pembroke Pines. By the third week, neighbors were paddling kayaks over. By the second month, more than 200 people were anchoring boats off the seawall every Saturday.

Five years later, the band still runs on that father-and-son core duo, with two original singles on Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon, an extended cast of vocalists and instrumentalists who fold in for bigger rooms, and a working repertoire of country, rock, and pop tuned for everything from a brewery courtyard to a vineyard wedding in the Blue Ridge.
